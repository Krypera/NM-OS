# Printing

NM-OS makes it easy to set up and use both local and network printers.

## Connecting a Printer

### USB Printers
For most modern USB printers, setup is automatic.
1. Turn on the printer.
2. Plug the USB cable into your computer.
3. NM-OS will detect the printer and install the necessary drivers in the background. A notification will let you know when it's ready.

### Network and Wi-Fi Printers
To connect to a printer over your local network:
1. Ensure the printer is turned on and connected to the same Wi-Fi or wired network as your computer.
2. Open the **Settings** app from the Activities overview.
3. Navigate to **Printers** in the left sidebar.
4. Click **Add Printer...** The system will scan your network and list available printers.
5. Select your printer and click **Add**.

## Printing a Document
In almost any application (like LibreOffice, your web browser, or the Document Viewer), you can print your work by pressing `Ctrl + P` or selecting **Print** from the application's menu.

## Managing Print Jobs
Once you've sent a document to the printer, a printer icon will appear in the top panel. You can click this icon to view the status of your print jobs, pause them, or cancel them.
