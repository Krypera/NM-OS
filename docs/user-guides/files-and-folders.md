# Files and Folders

Managing your documents, pictures, and downloads in NM-OS is simple and straightforward.

## The Files Application
Your home for all your data is the **Files** application. You can find it in the Activities overview or on your Dock.

### Your Home Folder
When you open Files, you start in your **Home** folder. This folder is private to you and contains standard folders like:
- **Documents:** For your text files, spreadsheets, and PDFs.
- **Downloads:** Where files saved from the internet go by default.
- **Pictures & Videos:** For your media files.

### Connecting USB Drives
When you plug in a USB flash drive or an external hard drive, it will automatically appear in the left sidebar of the Files application.
Click its name to open it. When you are done, be sure to click the **Eject** icon next to its name before physically unplugging it to prevent data loss.

### Using the Vault
If you enabled the **Vault** during setup (or later in the Control Center), you will see a special locked folder.
Any files placed inside the Vault are encrypted. When the Vault is locked, these files are completely inaccessible until you unlock it with your password.
