# Getting Started with NM-OS

Welcome to NM-OS! This system is designed to be as easy to use as a phone, while keeping your data safe.

## The Desktop
Your desktop is clean by design. At the top you'll find the panel, which gives you quick access to the time, system settings, and power controls.

At the bottom (or on the side, depending on your personalization settings) is the **Dock**. The Dock holds your favorite applications. You can add an app to the Dock by right-clicking its icon while it's running and selecting "Pin to Dash".

## Finding Applications
Press the **Super key** (often looks like a Windows logo or Command key) on your keyboard, or click the Activities button at the top left. This opens the Activities Overview.
Here you can simply start typing to find applications, files, or settings.

## Control Center
The **Control Center** is where you personalize your computer and manage your security.
You can change your wallpaper, switch between light and dark themes, and configure your network.
You can also change your Security Profile at any time from here.

## Help & Support
If you get stuck, look for the **System Help** app in your application list. It contains guidance for everyday tasks.
