# Internet and Email

NM-OS comes with safe and fast applications for staying connected.

## Web Browsing
During setup, you chose a default web browser. You can always change this later in the **Control Center -> Applications**.

- **Firefox**: A great choice for privacy and everyday browsing. If you enable "Tor-first" networking, Firefox seamlessly routes your traffic over the Tor network.
- **Chromium**: A fast, standard browser built on the same core as Chrome.

### Tor-First Networking
If your security profile is set to **Tor-first**, or if you manually enabled it in the Control Center, NM-OS will send your network traffic over the Tor network. This makes it very difficult for websites to track your physical location. 

*Note: Browsing might feel a bit slower when Tor-first is on. This is normal because your connection is bouncing around the world to protect your identity.*

## Email
You can read your email in the browser using your provider's website, or you can use the built-in email applications.

- **Thunderbird**: A powerful email client. Great if you have multiple accounts or need advanced filtering.
- **Geary**: A simpler, easy-to-use mail app. Great for quickly checking your messages.

To set up your email, open either app and enter your email address and password. The app will automatically figure out the correct settings for most major providers like Gmail, Yahoo, and Outlook.
